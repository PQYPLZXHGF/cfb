\biohead{Mary Boyd Clark}{}

Mary Boyd Clark was born on 13 March 1774 in Stewarton, Ayrshire, Scotland to Robert Clark (\p{Robert_Clark}) and Margaret Boyd (\p{Margaret_Boyd}).

She married Joseph Gilmour on 8 July 1797 in Fenwick, Ayrshire, Scotland \cite{MBClarkMarriage} and their marriage is written in the parish records as ``Joseph Gilmour in Parish of Kilmarnock and Mary Clark in this Parish gave up their names for Proclamation in order to Marriage said day''). They had seven children (see \p{Joseph_Gilmour}).

She died before 1870 in Hurlford, Ayrshire.\cite{JosephGilmourDeath}
