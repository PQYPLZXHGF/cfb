\biohead{Mary Boyd Clark}{}

Mary Boyd Clark was born on 13 March 1774 in Stewarton, Ayrshire, Scotland to Robert Clark and Margaret Boyd.

She married Joseph Gilmour on 8 July 1797 in Fenwick, Ayrshire, Scotland \cite{MBClarkMarriage} (showing in the parish records as "Joseph Gilmour in Parish of Kilmarnock and Mary Clark in this Parish gave up their names for Proclamation in order to Marriage said day") and they had seven children. (see\p{Joseph_Gilmour})

She died before 1870 in Hurlford, Ayrshire.\cite{JosephGilmourDeath}
