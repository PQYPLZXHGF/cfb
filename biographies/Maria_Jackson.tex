\biohead{Maria Jackson}{}
\index{Jackson, Maria}

Maria Jackson was born in May 1815 to March Jackson (\p{March_Jackson}) and Ann (\p{Ann_Holmes}) Jackson. She married John Moulsdale (\p{John_Moulsdale}) in April 1844 at St.Michaels, Liverpool, and they had three children:
Mary Ellen Mouldsdale (\p{Mary_Ellen_Moulsdale}), Sarah Ann Moulsdale (1851--?) and Maria Moulsdale (1857--?).
She died in 1863 \cite{MariaJacksonDeath}.
