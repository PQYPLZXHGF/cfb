\biohead{Maria Jackson}{}{}
\index{Jackson, Maria}

Maria Jackson was born in May 1815 to \bioref{March_Jackson} and \bioref{Ann_Holmes}.
She married \bioref{John_Moulsdale} in April 1844 at St. Michaels, Liverpool, and they had three children:
\bioref{Mary_Ellen_Moulsdale}, Sarah Ann Moulsdale (1851--?) and Maria Moulsdale (1857--?).
She died in 1863 \cite{MariaJacksonDeath}.
