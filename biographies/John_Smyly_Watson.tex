\biohead{John Smyly Watson}{}

John Smyly Watson was born on 5 June 1920 in	Sunbury-on-Thames, Surrey, to Erskine Watson (\p{Erskine_Watson}) and
Margery Munday (\p{Margery_Munday}). He had one sister:  Rosemary Elizabeth Watson (\p{Rosemary_Elizabeth_Watson}).
He married Moira ? (1921--2014) and they had three children: Andrew, William and Lucy.

He died on 16 July 2007 in Wimbledon, Surrey.
