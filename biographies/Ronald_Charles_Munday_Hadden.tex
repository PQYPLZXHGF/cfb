\biohead{Ronald Charles Munday Hadden}{}

Ronald Charles Munday Hadden was born in 1917 to Charles Brown Hadden (\p{Charles_Brown_Hadden}) and Mildred Mary Munday (\p{Mildred_Mary_Munday}). Until he was four years old they lived in Java before returning to live in England. 
He died in Exeter on 13 October 1993.\cite{RCMHaddenDeath}
