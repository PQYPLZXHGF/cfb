\index{Hadden, Charles Brown}

Charles Brown Hadden was born on 26 August 1917 in Jakarta, Indonesia.  He married Mildred Mary Munday (\p{Mildred_Mary_Munday}) on 5 December 1914\cite{JHMbible,FlickrJohnHillTree} and they had one son, Ronald Charles Munday Hadden (\p{Ronald_Charles_Munday_Hadden}).

He had an import/export and timber business, based in Java.

He died on 13 October 1993 in Exeter, Devon.\cite{CharlesBrownHaddenDeath}
