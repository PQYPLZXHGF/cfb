\biohead{Benjamin Browne}{}

Benjamin Browne lived in Somerset and married Sarah Harris.  They had four children: Jemima Browne (\p{Jemima_Browne}), Benjamin Browne, John Browne and Katherine Browne.

According to notes made by John Hill Munday in 1881, held by a family member and taken (\p{John_Hill_Munday} from a conversation with his Aunts Kate and Elizabeth, (who were the daughters of Benjamin's daughter Jemima),  Benjamin was a Maltster in 1798, working near Bath in Somerset.
