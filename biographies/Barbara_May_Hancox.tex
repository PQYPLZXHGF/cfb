\biohead{Barbara May Hancox}{}

Barbara Hancox was born on 25 April 1916 in Wallasey, Cheshire to Charles Edward Hancox \bioref{Charles_Edward_Hancox} and Alice Margaret Renner \bioref{Alice_Margaret_Renner}\cite{BarbaraHancoxBirth}.  She had four siblings: Charles Stanley Hancox \bioref{Charles_Stanley_Hancox}, Winifred Margaret Hancox \bioref{Winifred_Margaret_Hancox}, Norman Merrett Hancox \bioref{Norman_Merrett_Hancox} and Philip Renner Hancox \bioref{Philip_Renner_Hancox}.

In June 1939, Barbara arrived in New York with her parents on the Mauretania  and travelled back to England arriving on 7 July, again on the Mauretania \cite{BarbaraHancoxTravel}.

She married Stanislaus Karpinski in 1947, and they had two children: Jan and Peter.
