\textbf{Nyria Denton-Barker (n\'{e}e Hancox)} (23 February 1918 -- 15 October 2004) was born in Bebington.\citeref{BMDIndex_JoanNyriaHancox_birth}

Aged 6 (c.1925) she attended \idx{Howell's School} Llandaff (in the Vale of Glamorgan, \idx{Wales}).\citeref{OralHistoryJDB2008}

In the mid-thirties she studied nursing at Guy's Hospital, where she was working as a Sister on the children's ward when, in 1938, she was evacuated out of London.

She died on 15 October 2004 in St Helens, Tasmania.
