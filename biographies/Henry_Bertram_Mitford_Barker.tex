\biohead{Henry Bertram Mitford Barker}{}

Henry was born in 1885 in Liverpool [HBMBarkerBirth],  to Thomas Henry Barker (\p{Thomas_Henry_Barker}) and Mary Ellen Moulsdale (\p{Mary_Ellen_Moulsdale}). He had six brothers,  James Denton Barker (\p{James_Denton_Barker}), Charles Frederick Strangways Barker (\p{Charles_Frederick_Strangeways_Barker}), Thomas Percy Conyers Barker (\p{Thomas_Percy_Conyers_Barker}), Francis Darcy Mead Barker (\p{Francis_Darcy_Mead_Barker}), William Danby Holt Barker (\p{William_Danby_Holt_Barker}), and Jonathan Tong Barker (\p{Jonathan_Tong_Barker}).
