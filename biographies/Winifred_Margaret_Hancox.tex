\biohead{Winifred Margaret Hancox}{WinifredMargaretHancox}{}

Winifred Hancox was born in December 1907 in \idx{Birkenhead}\cite{BMD1907} and christened on 12 January the following year at St Mary's in Liscard.\cite{ParishReg} She was the second child (and first daughter) of Charles (\p{Charles_Edward_Hancox}) and Alice Hancox (\p{Alice_Margaret_Renner}), and in April 1911 the family was living at 54 Manor Road in Liscard, Cheshire.\cite{1911Census} Three more siblings were still to come. Her father died in 1952; her mother, 1977.

Winifred married twice: Donal Nicholas and Eric Langdon. She died before 2011.
