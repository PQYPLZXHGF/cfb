\biohead{Winifred Margaret Hancox}{}

Winifred Hancox was born in 12 January 1908 in \idx{Birkenhead}\cite{BMD1907}[WinifredHancoxBirth] and christened on 12 January the following year at St Mary's in Liscard.\cite{ParishReg}[WinifredHancoxBirth]. She was the second child (and first daughter) of Charles (\p{Charles_Edward_Hancox}) and Alice Hancox (\p{Alice_Margaret_Renner}), and in April 1911 the family was living at 54 Manor Road in Liscard, Cheshire.\cite{1911Census}[WinifredHancoxResidence].
Winifred married twice: Donal Nicholas and Eric Langdon. She died before 2011 [WinifredHancoxMarriagesDeath].
