\biohead{Vera Maunder}{}

In 1923, she left Kalgoorlie (where she had been working as a teacher) to transfer to the West Leederville State School.\cite{VMkal}

She married Ralph Munday (\p{Ralph_Munday}) in Java\cite{LadiesSection} and they adopted a daughter, Julia. In 1939 they returned to live in Cottesloe, Western Australia.
