\biohead{Elizabeth}{}

Elizabeth (maiden name unknown) was born in about 1812 in Fairford, Gloucestershire, England.\cite{Census1861Merrett}

She married James Merrett (\p{James_Merrett}) and they had seven children:
William Merrett;
Elizabeth Sarah Merrett;
Maria Mary Merrett (\p{Maria_Mary_Merrett});
Catherine M. Merrett;
Lucy Merrett;
Richard H. Merrett; and
Charlotte Merrett.
