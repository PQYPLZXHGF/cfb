\biohead{Alexander Browne}{Browne, Alexander}{}

Alexander Browne lived in \idx{County Down}. His wife's name is unkown but she may have been a Wallace. They had at least one daughter: \bioref{Charlotte_Wallace_Brown})

He lived in \idx{Ballynahinch}, Co.\ Down, and may have worked as a linen draper.

He died on 12 March 1827.
