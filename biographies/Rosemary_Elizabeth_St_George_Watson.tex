Rosemary Elizabeth St George Watson was born on	24 August 1916 	to Erskine Watson (\p{Erskine_Watson}) and Margery Munday (\p{Margery_Munday}). She had one brother:  John Smyly Watson (\p{John_Smyly_Watson}).

She married (unknown) Bond and they had two children: Dorian and D'Este.

She died on 28 September 2007.
