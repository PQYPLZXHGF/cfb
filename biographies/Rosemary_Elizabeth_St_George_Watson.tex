\biohead{Rosemary Elizabeth Watson}{}

Rosemary Elizabeth Watson was born on	24 August 1916 to Erskine Philip Watson (known as Pip) (\p{Erskine_Watson}) and Margery Munday (\p{Margery_Munday}). She had one brother:  John Smyly Watson (\p{John_Smyly_Watson}).

She married Hedleigh St. George Bond in 1939 at St. Luke's Church, Chelsea,  and they had two children: Dorian and D'Este. They divorced in 1951. 

She died on 13 November 2007.
