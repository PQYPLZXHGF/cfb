The C. F. Barker Archives

Genealogical research about the ancestors of Ralph and Nyria Denton-Barker and the descendants of their grandparents (the father of one of whom was C. F. Barker).
download the tree download the book

This is the home of our family archives. From here, you can download the tree (which is a single A3 sheet displaying everyone) and the book (which is an A4 format suitable for printing).

We invite you to contribute to either via Github.

Also, all photographs (some of which appear in the book above) are online in the photo albums.

For more information, contact sam@samwilson.id.au
